PURPOSE: Prompt template for identifying architectural patterns

Content structure:
- System role (software architect reviewing codebase)
- Task (identify design patterns and explain their purpose)
- Patterns to look for (MVC, Factory, Singleton, Observer, etc.)
- Required output format:
  * Pattern name
  * Where it's implemented
  * Why it's likely used here
  * How well it's implemented
- Constraints (only identify patterns you're confident about)

Variables to template in:
- {code_snippet}
- {class_definitions}
- {module_structure}
